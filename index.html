Memory Crash!

Welcome to Memory Crash, a memory card game. Find all matching pairs of cards with the same background color to win. To get on the score board match all the cards in the fewest amount of guesses. The more cards you play with, the more weight your score gets! To start, pick any even number from 4 to 100 as the number of cards you want to play with, then click "PLAY". You can reset a game any time by clicking on "RESET". Have fun!
